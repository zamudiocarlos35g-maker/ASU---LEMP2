Welcome to the "Tactile & Teamwork" Classroom!

Hello! My name is Carlos, and I am committed to creating an inclusive, safe, and highly structured learning environment. I come to this profession after working full-time as a paraeducator for students with visual impairments, has given me a deep, hands-on understanding of the needs and incredible potential of every child. I am also currently working toward my special education degree. I believe education is a team effort, and my goal is to foster **independence, critical thinking, and strong self-advocacy skills** in every student. I look forward to working together to ensure a supportive and exciting year of growth!
